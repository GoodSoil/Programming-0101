• Computer programs are all about information - how we gather it, store it, retreive it, and re-shape it.
  • What is information? What does it look like?
    • By information, we mean some kind of knowledge.
    • Information is something we can share and communicate with each other.
    • It can be complex, and it can be broken down into smaller, simpler pieces.
    • Simple (or "primitive") information comes in various forms: textual, numeric, conceptual.
